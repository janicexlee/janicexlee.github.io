Airbeatz

Music rhythm game with the LeapMotion sensor
Spring 2019



Airbeatz is a music rhythm game a friend and I created. You can select different songs to play, as well as difficulties. During the game play, there are 4 side bars, and gems come to each of those bars from the center. The player needs to tap the bars according to the beat of the song. You can view how accurately you matched the beat (Perfect, Good, or Miss), and combos if you get 5 hits or more in a row. At the end, you receive a score and total number of Perfect, Good, Misses, etc., and an overall letter grade of your performance.




Languages and tools: Python, Kivy, LeapMotion sensor

Green Streets Initiative

Company Dashboard
Winter 2016, Spring 2017

In order to give companies in Green Street's Corporate Challenge a better understanding of their impact on Walk/Ride Day and better control over their employees’ participation, we made an employer interface where each employer can access their employees’ check-in survey data, as well as overall statistics for each month and each year. Some statistics include percentage of employee participation, total calories burned, total carbon dioxide emitted and saved, modes of transportation and total duration traveled for each mode for both Walk-Ride Day and Normal Days, and graphs of progress over the past year.
